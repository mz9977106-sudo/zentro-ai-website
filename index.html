Frequently Asked Questions

How much advance payment is required? +
We require only 30% advance before starting any project. The remaining 70% is paid after delivery.
How long does a project take? +
Delivery time depends on project complexity. Simple websites take 1 week, advanced projects 2-4 weeks.
Do you provide support after delivery? +
Yes, we provide post-delivery support and maintenance for all clients.
Do you offer discounts? +
Repeat clients receive special discounts. Contact us on WhatsApp for current offers.
How do I get a quote? +
Fill out the Project Requirements form on this page and submit it. We will review your requirements and send you a custom quote within 24 hours. You can also reach us directly on WhatsApp for a faster response.
Can I request custom features? +
Yes, absolutely. Every project we build is custom. You can describe any specific features you need in the Project Requirements form, and we will include them in your quote.
Will my website work on mobile devices? +
Yes. All websites we build are fully mobile-friendly and responsive. They work correctly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
Can my website be updated in the future? +
Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance and update services. After your website is delivered, you can contact us anytime to request changes, new features, or content updates.
Do you provide WhatsApp integration? +
Yes. We can integrate WhatsApp contact buttons, WhatsApp ordering links, and WhatsApp chat widgets into your website so your customers can reach you instantly.
Can I connect my own domain? +
Yes. We can connect your existing domain to your new website, or help you purchase and set up a new domain as part of the project.
